Snowball


What if your phone could be a social robot—or even your pet?

Developed in Swift and Procreate, this was a fun IOS app I developed with my team at nwHacks 2021. See the devpost.

We wanted to design a low-maintenance virtual pet that users could physically interact with to reduce feelings of isolation and loneliness. The more you play with Snowball, the more his happiness snowballs! Here are some of the app's happy features:


Swipping + Tapping


Shaking the device


Not petting Snowball


Although it's not shown here, Snowball also has a voice recognition feature where he will respond if you call his name!
